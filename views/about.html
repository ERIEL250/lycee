Our History

Lycee de Nyanza, nestled in the Southern Province of Rwanda, stands as a beacon of educational excellence since its establishment in 2005 with just two classes and 70 students. From these humble beginnings, it has grown into a bastion of academic prowess and cultural enrichment in the region. Evolving into a vibrant academic community, it fosters intellectual curiosity and innovation, emphasizing holistic education to nurture the talents and aspirations of its diverse student body. Through a comprehensive curriculum and dedicated faculty, Lycee de Nyanza inspires generations of learners to strive for excellence and make positive contributions to their communities and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our vision at Lycee de Nyanza is to be a leading institution in vocational and technical education, fostering innovation, excellence, and leadership to shape a better future for our students and community.

Our vision

Lycee de Nyanza's mission is to empower students through high-quality TVET education in fields like software development, automobile, and culinary arts, cultivating skilled, innovative leaders for the modern world.
